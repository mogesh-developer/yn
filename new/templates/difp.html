Diploma in Food Production (DIFP)

Step Into the Culinary World with Confidence

Course Overview

This course teaches food preparation techniques, kitchen operations, and culinary science. Ideal for aspiring chefs and kitchen professionals.

Course Duration

1 Year

Course Modules

  • Basics of Culinary Arts
  • Indian and Continental Cuisine
  • Bakery and Confectionery
  • Food Presentation & Plating
  • Inventory and Store Management
  • Kitchen Safety and Hygiene

Eligibility

10th or 10+2 from a recognized board.

Career Opportunities

  • Commis Chef
  • Line Cook
  • Food Prep Assistant
  • Kitchen Steward
