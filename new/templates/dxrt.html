Diploma in X-Ray Technology (DXRT)

Be a Part of Diagnostic Innovation

Course Overview

This course provides students with the essential skills needed to operate X-ray machines and work in diagnostic imaging departments. It covers the theory, techniques, and safety aspects of X-ray technology.

Course Duration

2 Years

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Radiology
  • X-Ray Imaging Techniques
  • Radiographic Safety and Protection
  • Medical Terminology and Anatomy
  • Radiation Physics
  • Clinical Practice and Procedures

Eligibility

Minimum Qualification: High School (10+2) or equivalent with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) stream.

Job Opportunities

Graduates can work as:

  • X-Ray Technician
  • Radiologic Technologist
  • Medical Imaging Specialist
  • Radiology Assistant
  • Healthcare Imaging Consultant
