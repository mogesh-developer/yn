Diploma in Catering and Hotel Management

Your Gateway to a Successful Career in the Hospitality Industry

Course Overview

This diploma provides you with all the essential skills to excel in the catering and hospitality industry. With a mix of theoretical learning and practical training, you'll be prepared to work in hotels, restaurants, resorts, and more.

Course Duration

12 Months (Including Internship)

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Hospitality & Tourism
  • Food Production and Culinary Skills
  • Food & Beverage Service Management
  • Housekeeping Management
  • Front Office Management
  • Event Planning & Catering
  • Internship in Leading Hotels & Restaurants

Eligibility

Minimum Qualification: High School (10+2) or equivalent

Job Opportunities

Graduates can work as:

  • Hotel Manager
  • Catering Manager
  • Restaurant Supervisor
  • Chef / Sous Chef
  • Event Coordinator
  • Front Desk Executive
