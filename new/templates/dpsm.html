Diploma in Pre School Management (DPSM)

Lead and Inspire in Early Childhood Education

Course Overview

This diploma focuses on preparing educators and managers for preschools. Learn child psychology, curriculum planning, and school operations management.

Course Duration

1 Year

Course Modules

  • Early Childhood Development
  • Classroom Management Techniques
  • Child Psychology and Counseling
  • Preschool Curriculum Planning
  • Parent-Teacher Communication
  • Preschool Administration

Eligibility

10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board.

Career Opportunities

  • Preschool Teacher
  • Childcare Center Manager
  • Montessori Educator
