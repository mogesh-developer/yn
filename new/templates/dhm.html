Diploma in Hospital Management (DHM)

Shape Your Future in the Healthcare Industry

Course Overview

This course will prepare students for a management role in the healthcare sector, focusing on the administration and operation of healthcare facilities such as hospitals, clinics, and medical institutions.

Course Duration

2 Years

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Healthcare Management
  • Health Systems and Policies
  • Human Resource Management in Healthcare
  • Financial Management in Healthcare
  • Healthcare Law and Ethics
  • Marketing and Patient Care in Healthcare

Eligibility

Minimum Qualification: High School (10+2) or equivalent in any stream.

Job Opportunities

Graduates can work as:

  • Hospital Administrator
  • Healthcare Manager
  • Medical Records Manager
  • Healthcare Consultant
  • Public Health Administrator
