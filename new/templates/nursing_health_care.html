Diploma in Nursing and Health Care

Building Compassionate Healthcare Professionals

Course Overview

This diploma provides students with the necessary knowledge and hands-on skills to pursue a career in nursing and healthcare. The program equips you to work in hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, and other healthcare facilities.

Course Duration

18 Months (Including Internship)

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Nursing & Healthcare
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Patient Care & Hygiene
  • First Aid & Emergency Response
  • Pharmacology & Medication Administration
  • Medical Ethics & Communication
  • Clinical Internship

Eligibility

Minimum Qualification: High School (10+2) with Science (Biology, Chemistry)

Job Opportunities

Graduates can work as:

  • Registered Nurse
  • Healthcare Assistant
  • Medical Secretary
  • Patient Care Coordinator
  • Health Educator
  • Clinical Research Assistant
