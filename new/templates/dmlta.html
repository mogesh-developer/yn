Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technician Assistant (DMLTA)

Prepare for a Career in Diagnostic Healthcare

Course Overview

This course trains students in performing laboratory tests that help diagnose diseases. You'll gain expertise in working with various lab equipment, preparing samples, and ensuring proper health diagnostics.

Course Duration

1 Year

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Medical Laboratory Technology
  • Blood Collection and Processing
  • Clinical Microbiology
  • Medical Lab Safety Practices
  • Biochemistry and Hematology
  • Laboratory Instrumentation

Eligibility

Minimum Qualification: High School (10+2) or equivalent with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) stream.

Job Opportunities

Graduates can work as:

  • Medical Laboratory Technician
  • Clinical Laboratory Assistant
  • Laboratory Technologist
  • Phlebotomist
  • Diagnostic Lab Assistant
