Course Overview

This diploma prepares students to provide basic care and assist registered nurses in hospitals, clinics, and home care. It covers patient hygiene, medical support, and health record maintenance.

Course Duration

1 Year

Course Modules

  • Basics of Nursing & Patient Care
  • Human Anatomy and Physiology
  • Medical Terminology
  • Infection Control Procedures
  • First Aid and Emergency Care
  • Hospital and Clinical Training

Eligibility

10th or 10+2 with a background in Science is preferred, but not mandatory.

Job Opportunities

  • Nursing Assistant
  • Home Health Aide
  • Hospital Attendant
  • Community Health Worker
  • Geriatric Care Assistant
