Diploma in General Duty Nursing Assistant (DGDA)

Empowering the Future of Healthcare

Course Overview

This course equips students with the necessary skills to assist in the day-to-day nursing and patient care in hospitals and clinics. You will learn basic medical and nursing skills to help healthcare professionals.

Course Duration

1 Year

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Healthcare
  • Basic Nursing Skills
  • Patient Care Techniques
  • Medical Terminology
  • Infection Control and Sterilization
  • Ethical and Legal Aspects of Nursing

Eligibility

Minimum Qualification: High School (10+2) or equivalent in any stream.

Job Opportunities

Graduates can work as:

  • General Duty Nursing Assistant
  • Patient Care Assistant
  • Healthcare Assistant
  • Medical Ward Assistant
  • Home Care Nurse
